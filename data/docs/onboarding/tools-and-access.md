# Tools and Access

This guide covers platform tools, support channels, and resolving common access issues for new traders.

## Trading Dashboard

The Trumid dashboard provides:

- **Live market data**: Level and trade prints, mid updates, and sector benchmarks for IG and HY
- **Portfolio view**: Positions, cost basis, DV01, and sector allocation with export to CSV
- **Analytics**: RFQ hit rate, Swarm participation, and counterparty response metrics

Customize layouts per desk; saved views sync across sessions when SSO is enabled.

## Getting Help

- **IT support**: VPN, login, hardware, and software access — use the IT Self-Service Assistant or `#it-help`
- **Trading questions**: Protocols, RFQ workflow, Swarm mechanics — desk lead or `#trading-onboarding`
- **Compliance**: Pre-trade blocks, registration, restricted lists — `compliance@trumid.com`

## Common Access Issues

- **Cannot log in**: Verify Okta MFA; clear browser cache; confirm you are on the corporate VPN
- **Missing market data**: Entitlements are desk-specific; open an IT ticket with your desk code
- **RFQ button disabled**: Usually indicates incomplete compliance attestation or expired counterparty list
- **Staging vs production**: Staging uses separate credentials; never mix order entry between environments

Most access requests are fulfilled within one business day after manager approval.
