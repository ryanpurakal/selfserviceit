# Trumid Platform Basics

Welcome to Trumid's electronic bond trading platform. This guide covers the core protocols and what to expect in your first week.

## Trading Protocols

Trumid supports three primary execution workflows:

- **RFQ (Request for Quote)**: Send targeted inquiries to approved counterparties for specific CUSIPs. Best for size discovery and negotiated pricing on less liquid names.
- **Portfolio Trading**: Execute baskets of bonds in a single workflow with netted economics. Use when rebalancing or rolling exposure across sectors.
- **Swarms**: Participate in timed liquidity events where multiple participants trade simultaneously at mid-market or improved levels. Ideal for high-velocity IG and crossover names.

## Dashboard Features

Your home dashboard shows live market data, watchlists, recent RFQs, and open Swarm invitations. The portfolio view summarizes positions, DV01, and P&L by sector. Analytics panels surface hit rates, average response times, and protocol mix.

## First-Week Checklist

- Complete compliance attestation and counterparty approvals
- Join a platform walkthrough with your desk lead
- Execute a supervised test RFQ in staging, then your first live trade with approval
- Configure alerts for Swarm invitations and RFQ responses
- Bookmark the onboarding assistant and compliance escalation contacts

Reach out to your desk lead or `#trading-onboarding` for protocol-specific questions.
