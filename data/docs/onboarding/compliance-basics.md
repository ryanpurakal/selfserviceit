# Compliance Basics for Traders

All trading activity on Trumid is subject to FINRA and firm policy. New traders must understand pre-trade controls before executing live orders.

## FINRA Requirements

Registered personnel must maintain current Series 57 (or applicable) registration, complete annual compliance training, and adhere to market conduct rules including fair dealing, best execution documentation, and communications retention. Personal trading in covered securities requires pre-clearance.

## Pre-Trade Compliance Checks

The platform enforces automated checks before order submission:

- **Counterparty approval**: Only approved legal entities and desk relationships may receive RFQs or accept trades
- **Trade size limits**: Per-name and per-desk notional caps; oversized orders route to compliance review
- **Insider information**: Restricted lists block trading in names where you have material non-public information

Failed checks display a clear reason code. Do not attempt to circumvent limits via order splitting.

## When to Escalate

Contact the Compliance desk immediately if you receive MNPI, observe unusual counterparty behavior, need an exception to size limits, or see surveillance alerts on your activity. For urgent pre-trade blocks during market hours, use the in-app **Escalate to Compliance** link or email `compliance@trumid.com`.

Document the CUSIP, intended size, counterparty, and business rationale when requesting an exception.
