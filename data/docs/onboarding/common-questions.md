# Common Questions — Trader Onboarding

Quick answers to frequently asked questions from new traders on the Trumid platform.

## How do I execute my first trade?

Start in **staging** with your desk lead. Submit a small RFQ on an approved IG CUSIP, review pre-trade compliance results, and accept a counterparty response. After successful staging, request live entitlements. Your first live trade requires manager approval in the workflow.

## Where do I find bond pricing data?

Live mids and recent trades appear on the instrument detail page and in your watchlist. For composite benchmarks, use the **Analytics → Market** tab. Historical prints export via CSV from the same panel. Pricing for illiquid names often comes via RFQ responses rather than displayed mids.

## How do I use Swarms?

Swarm invitations appear in the notification bell and on the dashboard **Swarms** tile. Review the event terms (size range, side, timing), confirm compliance pre-checks, and submit interest before the window closes. Participation is optional; declining does not affect your RFQ standing.
