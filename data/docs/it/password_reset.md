# Password Reset Instructions

## Resetting Your Password

1. Go to `password.trumid.com`.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your Trumid email address.
4. Check your inbox for the reset link (it expires in 24 hours).
5. Follow the link and create a new password that meets the requirements below.
6. Sign out of all active Okta sessions and sign back in with the new password.

## Password Requirements

- Minimum 12 characters.
- At least one uppercase letter.
- At least one lowercase letter.
- At least one number.
- At least one special character (`! @ # $ % ^ & *`).
- Cannot reuse any of your last 5 passwords.
- Cannot contain your name or username.

## Locked Account

If your account is locked after 5 failed login attempts:
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock, **or**
- Contact IT immediately for a manual unlock if it's blocking critical work.

## MFA / Okta Verify Issues

- Lost phone? Contact IT immediately to deactivate the old factor and enroll a new one.
- Push notifications not arriving? Open the Okta Verify app and pull down to refresh, or use the 6-digit code instead.
